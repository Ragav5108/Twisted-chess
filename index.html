🎮 Game Rules

🔄 Piece Changes:

  • 👑 King: Moves like a Queen - any number of squares in any direction
  • ♛ Queen: Moves like a King - only 1 square in any direction
  • ♜ Rook, ♝ Bishop, ♞ Knight, ♙ Pawn: Standard chess moves

🎯 Winning:

  • Put the opponent's King in Check (under attack)
  • If they cannot escape = Checkmate = You Win!
  • Capturing the King also wins

⚠️ Note: Castling and en passant are disabled for simplicity.
